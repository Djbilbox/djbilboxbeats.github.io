Who is DJBILBOX BEATS?

Pro sound, zero gatekeeping.

Behind DJBILBOX BEATS is Bilel Abdelkader Attalah — producer, beatmaker and sound designer from Toulouse. For years he's been shaping a signature sound across Oriental, Trap, Drill, G-Funk, Afrotrap & House, and sharing it with artists everywhere.

The mission is simple: give every artist studio-grade tools without the studio price. Beats are free-for-profit with instant licensing, the VST plugins bring real signature sounds to any DAW, and the drum kits are built from the very sounds used on his own records. 🐪🎧
