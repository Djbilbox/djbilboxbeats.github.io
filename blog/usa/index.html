FAQ

Can I use these beats commercially in the US?

Yes. Every free beat includes an unlimited commercial license — you can release, monetize and distribute your song across the US and worldwide. For full ownership, an exclusive license with stems is available.

Do I have to credit DJBILBOX BEATS?

No, credit isn't required. A tag like "Prod. by DJBILBOX BEATS" is appreciated and helps support future free releases, but it's optional.

What formats do I get?

High-quality MP3 or WAV instantly. Exclusive buyers also receive full stems and project files.

Do the VST plugins work on my DAW?

They run as VST3 and Standalone on Windows and macOS — compatible with FL Studio, Ableton, Logic, Studio One and more.
